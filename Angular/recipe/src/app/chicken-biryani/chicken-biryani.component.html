Delicious Chicken Biryani

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 2 large onions, sliced
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tsp biryani masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Cooking oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Soak the rice for 20 minutes, then drain.
  2. Heat oil, sauté onions until golden brown. Set half aside.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste to the remaining onions and sauté briefly.
  4. Add chicken, biryani masala, turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until chicken is browned.
  5. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  6. Add yogurt and simmer until chicken is tender.
  7. In another pot, boil water with salt, add rice, and cook until 70% done. Drain.
  8. Layer rice over the chicken mixture, top with fried onions and coriander.
  9. Cover and cook on low heat for 20 minutes.
  10. Fluff gently and serve hot.
