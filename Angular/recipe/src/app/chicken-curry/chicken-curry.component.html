Delicious Chicken Curry

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 large onions, finely chopped
  • 3 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1/4 cup cooking oil
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 2 teaspoons red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add chopped onions, and sauté until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
  3. Stir in chicken pieces and cook until they are no longer pink.
  4. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  5. Pour in the tomato puree and cook until oil separates from the masala.
  6. Whisk yogurt and add it to the chicken. Cook until the chicken is tender.
  7. Sprinkle garam masala and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  8. Serve hot with rice or naan.
