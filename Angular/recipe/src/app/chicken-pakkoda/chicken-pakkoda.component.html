Delicious Chicken Pakkoda

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken breast, cut into small pieces
  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • Water, as needed
  • Oil for deep frying
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix gram flour, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, coriander powder, cumin powder, and salt.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste, green chilies, and water to form a thick batter.
  3. Add chicken pieces to the batter and mix well to coat them evenly.
  4. Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
  5. Fry the chicken pieces in batches until they are golden brown and crispy.
  6. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  8. Serve hot with mint chutney or ketchup.
