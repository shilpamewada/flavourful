Delicious Veg noodles

<

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, potatoes), diced
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 2 tsp biryani masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 cups water
  • Fresh cilantro and mint leaves, chopped
  • Saffron strands soaked in 2 tbsp warm milk (optional)
  • Fried onions and cashews for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the rice until the water runs clear. Soak in water for 30 minutes, then drain.
  2. Heat ghee or oil in a large pot. Add sliced onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Add garlic, ginger, and mixed vegetables. Cook for a few minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender.
  4. Add chopped tomato, yogurt, biryani masala, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for a few minutes.
  5. Add the drained rice and water. Stir to combine.
  6. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low. Cover and simmer until the rice is cooked and the water is absorbed, about 20 minutes.
  7. Garnish with fresh cilantro, mint leaves, saffron milk, fried onions, and cashews before serving.
