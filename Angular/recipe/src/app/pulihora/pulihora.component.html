Delicious Pulihora

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1/4 cup tamarind pulp
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 dried red chilies
  • 1/4 cup peanuts
  • 1/4 tsp asafoetida (hing)
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook rice and let it cool.
  2. Soak tamarind pulp in warm water, extract juice, and set aside.
  3. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  4. Add cumin seeds, dried red chilies, peanuts, asafoetida, curry leaves, turmeric, and green chilies. Saut√© for a minute.
  5. Add tamarind juice and salt. Cook until the oil separates.
  6. Mix the tamarind mixture with the cooked rice.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve.
