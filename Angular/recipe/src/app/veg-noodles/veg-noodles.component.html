Delicious Veg noodles

Ingredients:

  • 200g noodles
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, bell peppers, cabbage, beans), julienned
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp chili sauce
  • 1 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 green onions, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Cook noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  3. Add sliced onion and minced garlic, saut√© until fragrant.
  4. Add mixed vegetables and stir-fry for a few minutes until tender-crisp.
  5. Add cooked noodles to the pan and toss well.
  6. Stir in soy sauce, chili sauce, tomato ketchup, and vinegar. Mix well to coat the noodles.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Garnish with chopped green onions and serve hot.
