Delicious Chicken Fried Rice

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 cup chicken breast, diced
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn)
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 green onions, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Heat 1 tbsp cooking oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add diced chicken and cook until no longer pink. Remove from pan and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add beaten eggs and scramble until cooked. Remove and set aside.
  4. Add remaining oil to the pan, saut√© garlic and onion until fragrant.
  5. Add mixed vegetables and cook for a few minutes until tender.
  6. Add cooked rice, chicken, and scrambled eggs to the pan.
  7. Pour in soy sauce and sesame oil, stirring well to combine.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Garnish with chopped green onions and serve hot.
